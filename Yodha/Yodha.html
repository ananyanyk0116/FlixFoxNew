Yodha

Yodha ( transl. Warrior) is a 2024 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film directed by Sagar Ambre and Pushkar Ojha, and produced by Dharma Productions. The film stars Sidharth Malhotra, Raashii Khanna and Disha Patani. After multiple release postponements, Yodha was theatrically released on 15 March 2024.

Marco

Marco is an upcoming Indian Malayalam-language neo-noir action thriller film produced by Shareef Muhammed under Cubes Entertainments and directed by Haneef Adeni. It is the spin-off of Adeni's 2019 film Mikhael. The film stars Unni Mukundan in the titular role, alongside an ensemble cast of Jagadish, Siddique, Anson Paul, Yukti Thareja, Riyaz Khan, Jinu Joseph, Sreejith Ravi and Kabir Duhan Singh. The music was composed by Ravi Basrur, while cinematography and editing were handled by Chandru Selvaraj and Shameer Muhammed.
